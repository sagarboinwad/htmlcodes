Key Website Design Issues

Sr No. Website Name Design Issues
1. Amazon
  • Fake Reviews (No clear way to filter out manipulated reviews).
  • Too many ads, recommendations, and pop-ups.
2. Khan Accademy
  • There is no dark mode option, which can cause eye strain during long study sessions.
  • While there is a forum, it lacks real-time discussions or live chat support for students.
3. Adobe Portfolio
  • No E-Commerce Support (cannot sell directly through the platform).
  • Users cannot fully customize layout or design.
4. Hindustan Times
  • Slow Page Load Speed (heavy content slows down browsing).
  • It provide too much information at once, making navigation difficult.
5. Simplilearn
  • The search and filtering system does not effectively highlight free content.
  • Certain buttons and menus are small or difficult to tap on smaller screens.



Conclusion

By evaluating various websites, we understand what works and what doesn't. A good website should be user-friendly, fast, and visually appealing while avoiding unnecessary distractions.
